How The Game Works

"Please wait while game is loading" Once the game has loaded and is on the main start up page, Hover your hand for a few seconds over the hand shaped button on screen.

In the next screen, select the game you want to play by hovering your hand over the the button with the name of the game you want to play.

The Maze Game is hard, so we have eased you in gently with level 1 which is a piece of cake... Right? At your start position, guide your ball with your hand to drag it through the maze, be careful though if you hit the edge of the maze then you go right back to the start and lose a life. If you lose all your 4 lives you end the game. Try get through the 3 levels with out dying, good luck, you'll need it!

The aim of Car Game is to wipe off the fog off the windscreen as quick as you can, once you have most of the fog off the window, try hovering your hand over the steering wheel to drive (but be carful, don't knock down Granny). The car will only drive if most of the fog has been cleared.
